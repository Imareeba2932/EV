Predictive Maintenance for EVs

Failure Probability & Smart Service

EVIoT Predict is a data-driven predictive maintenance solution built for electric vehicles. It specializes in estimating the probability of component failure using IoT-enabled telemetry data such as voltage, temperature, and vibration. By providing accurate, early warnings, the system helps minimize unexpected breakdowns, reduce downtime, and enhance overall fleet reliability
